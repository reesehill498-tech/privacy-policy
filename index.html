4. Data Deletion & Retention

Since we do not manage user accounts or cloud servers, you have complete control over your data retention.

  • Delete Single Items: You can delete individual customers, orders, or fabrics within the app interface.
  • Clear All Data: The "Settings > Danger Zone > Clear All App Data" option instantly wipes the local database. This action is permanent.
  • Uninstalling: Uninstalling the app from your device will permanently remove all locally stored data, including images and database records, as they are stored in the app-specific sandbox.
