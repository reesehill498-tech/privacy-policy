Privacy Policy — StitchVault

Last Updated: November 25, 2025

1. Core Principle: Offline-First, Local-Only Storage

StitchVault is designed as an offline boutique management app. We do not collect, transmit, store, or share any user data on external servers, cloud services, or third-party platforms. All information entered into the App is stored only on your device in a local database.

2. Information Stored Locally on Your Device

The App functions as a business management tool and stores data only for your own use. This may include personal information about your customers that you manually enter.

Data Stored Locally

Categories of locally stored data
Category Type of Data Purpose How It’s Collected Storage Location
Customer Information Name, phone number, email address, physical address Contacting customers and managing orders Entered by the user Local device database (SQLite/Drift)
Business Records Fabric inventory (SKU, quantity, price), order details, prices, order status Core boutique management features Entered by the user Local device database
Images Photos of fabrics and order items Visual inventory management Captured or selected by user Local app folder on the device

The App does not automatically collect information. All data is added manually by the user (boutique owner).

3. Data Collection & Sharing (Google Play Data Safety)

Category Disclosure
Data Collection No data is collected by the developer.
Data Sharing None. Data is not shared with any third parties.
Data Transmission The App does not send data over the internet.
Data Processing All calculations and processing occur locally on the device (e.g., totals, statistics, sorting).

4. Permissions Used by the App

Camera & Photos/Media

Used only to allow the user to take or select images of fabrics or products. Images remain stored locally on the device and are never uploaded anywhere.

Manage External Storage (Android)

Used only when the user chooses to export a backup (database + images) to a folder on their device. The App does not access unrelated files. The App does not upload backups to any cloud or server.

5. Data Security & User Control

Security

All data is protected by the device’s operating system. The developer does not have access to your data.

Full User Control

You may delete your data at any time using the in-app controls or by uninstalling the App:

  • “Delete Customer”, “Delete Fabric,” or “Delete Order” buttons inside the App.
  • “Clear All App Data” in App Settings (resets the local database).
  • Uninstalling the App (removes database and images permanently).

6. Children’s Privacy

This App is intended for adult business owners and is not designed for children under 13. We do not knowingly collect any data from children.

7. Policy Updates

This Privacy Policy may be updated occasionally. Any changes will be posted inside the App or on the official listing before they take effect.

8. Contact Us

For questions or concerns regarding this Privacy Policy, please contact us at the developer email address provided on the Google Play Store listing.
